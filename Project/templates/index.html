  • All our cars are registered and approved by Luther College Security to ensure the safety of our users. Register your car with NorseHorse to offer rides at your own rate. Help a friend... earn a little.
  • NorseHorse exclusively serves Luther College students, faculty and staff, thus requiring a luther.edu email extension for registration. Our rideshare app is designed to cater for the transportation need both to and from the college.
  • NorseHorse rideshare app is free and easy to use. The webapp user interface makes it easy to schedule rides, view requests and offers and creates prompt user notifications.
  • NorseHorse organizes rides from Luther College to airpots and key travel points around the Midwest such as Minneapolis, Chicago, Madison and Des Moines. We also organize shopping shuttles to nearby retail stores like WalMart.
